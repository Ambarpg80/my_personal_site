About Me
I'm a work in progress and currently studying software engineering at Flatiron School. IT is a second career for me after I tried my hand in performing arts for some time. I transitioned to IT in 2017 when I received the opprtunity to do classes in Network Administration with New Horizons. I earned my COMPTIA A+, Network+ & Security+ certifications and have worked in Application Support and IT Support. The work intorduced me to the IT industry and the different roles available, but I still needed to find my niche. I pivoted to web development because I realized that I like working on products. I enjoyed the collaborative culture and creativity fostered by the performing arts and see some of that in web development.
